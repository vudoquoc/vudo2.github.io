About Me

I am currently a PhD candidate in Mechanical Engineering at UIUC under professor Kyle Smith. Before UIUC, I obtained my B. Eng in Aerospace Engineering at Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2018. During my undergraduate at HUST, I was a modeling guy interested in computational fluid dynamics (CFD) and creating simulation tools for fluid-structure interaction (FSI) problems. After joining UIUC, I switched gear to become an experimentalist. My current research focuses on developing new materials for water treatment applications. In my free time, I enjoy listening to music, singing, playing guitar, taking photographs, watching scientific documentaries and anime.
